Tiers

There are six main tiers for pokemon compative battling going from strongest to weakest

Uber

In the uber tier there are mainly legendarys, Megas and pokemon with awesome abilties. Legendarys like arceaus are in uber tier. With megas like mega lucario, Mega gengar and Mega Blaziken. Then pokemon like greninja have awesome abbilties like protein which changes the users type depending on the moves type.

OU

This is the tier everyone uses its the main tier.Most megas are in this tier also lots of pokemon with wide movepool making them very unprdictible this tier is home to evolite chansey and where u-turning landours-therian is. I suggest using two walls a phiscal and special also have a mega and a choice item mon. Ou stand for overused.

UU

UU is one of the most diverse tiers. About 30% of the megas are here (with 50% in OU)it has some of the weaker legendarys. Its got a lot of fan favioutes like Gardivour, Lucario and Infernape. UU stands for Underused.

RU

RU is a tier with a lot of secondary evolations in them. In the Tier there are 2 megas and 5 starters. This tier is full of pokemon who can have there job done better by other mons. It also has some fan favioutes like blastoosie, Typhlosioan, Aeroductal and scarafty. RU stands for Rarly used.

NU

NU is my favioute tier with my Two favioute starters, Torterra and Sammaroutt. Also my favioute staller and the only mega in this tier audiono. Fan Favioutes include Elective, Charizard and Magmorter. It also has a lot of forgotton but still cool mons like Golurk, Gastrodon, Zangoose and Rhydon. NU stands for Never used.

LC

LC standing for little cup is a tier where all the pokemon are the first forms of other mons. They are all level 5 therefore changing alot by making attack much more useful then defense as you have such little HP.What you want are pokemon who had Single forms in Gen1 and got evoloutions later because they have slightly higher stats. You will also want a baby psuedo Legendary because ther also have higher stats because the evolve so late. Pokemon like ghastly and abra are also really good in this tier.
